Research Projects

'Shifting Landscapes of Healing: Health Cosmology, Ethnomedicine and Experiential Health in Bhotia Highlands' March 2025
Researcher · Munsiyari, Uttarakhand, India
  • Led a mixed-method study examining how cultural beliefs and healthcare systems shape treatment choices and health outcomes in Himalayan communities through ethnographic study and quantitative analysis.
  • Conducted case studies, in-depth interviews, life history and focus group discussions to understand experiential health perspectives and assess access and utilization of healthcare services and local healing practices.
  • Generated actionable insights on medical pluralism, local health governance, and cultural determinants of care, contributing to evidence-based approaches for inclusive health policy.
'An Anthropological Account on Kamand Valley' February 2023
Researcher · Mandi, Himachal Pradesh, India
  • Conducted mixed-method field research to assess social organization, caste, economy, and health systems in rural Himalayan communities.
  • Collected and analyzed quantitative demographic and biological data alongside qualitative ethnographic insights to develop a comprehensive community profile.
  • Identified key developmental challenges and cultural determinants influencing education, livelihood, and healthcare access, and suggested evidence-based rural development strategies.
Ethnographic Film — 'Hanging by a Thread' Mar 2022 – Sep 2022
Visual Anthropologist · Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India
  • Conceptualized and developed the research framework and visual narrative for an ethnographic film exploring local livelihoods and cultural practices.
  • Planned and managed logistics, ethical protocols, and rapport building with participants to ensure culturally sensitive data collection.
  • Conducted qualitative interviews and visual documentation, translating field insights into a coherent ethnographic story.
  • Edited and produced the final film using DaVinci Resolve, integrating analytical depth with visual storytelling.
  • Selected and screened at the World Anthropology Congress IUAES 2023.
